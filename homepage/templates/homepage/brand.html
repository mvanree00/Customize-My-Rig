Will you be overclocking? 

Overclocking allows you to push your GPU/CPU over its designed limits, increasing performance. This reduces part lifespan, requires more energy, and may void warranties.

      


Water or air cooling? 

Water cooling removes heat better than air cooling; however, water cooling is considerably more expensive.
