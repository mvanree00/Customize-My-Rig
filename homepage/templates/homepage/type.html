What type of PC are you interested in?


Gaming PC 
If you plan on using your computer primarily for video games, you will want to spend more on the GPU and CPU.

Gaming & Streaming PC 
Similar to a gaming PC with more of an emphasis on CPU quality and RAM quantity.

Production PC 
If you plan on doing processor heavy tasks like video editing, 3D modeling, or game development, you will want to spend more on CPU, RAM, and GPU.
